About Us

Ahad Youth was founded on 14th August 2018, and since then, it has continued to grow with dedication, faith, and unity. Our main purpose is to do good deeds for the sake of Allah and to serve the community, especially by helping our local masjid. Ahad Youth works with sincerity, teamwork, and kindness to spread positive messages among people. The group focuses on organizing Islamic programs for children, where they can learn about Islam, enjoy activities, and grow spiritually. During these events, we also provide gifts to children to bring happiness and encourage them to do good.
